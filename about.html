About us

Ponderosa Retreat was founded by a passionate team of nature enthusiasts and wellness advocates dedicated to creating a sanctuary for those seeking rejuvenation and connection. Inspired by the breathtaking landscapes surrounding the retreat, they envisioned a space where people could escape the demands of daily life and immerse themselves in the healing power of nature.

The team believes in the transformative power of nature and the importance of fostering genuine connections. They strive to create an environment where guests can unwind, explore their creativity, and build lasting friendships. Ponderosa Retreat stands as a testament to their vision, inviting all to find peace and inspiration amidst the beauty of the great outdoors.
