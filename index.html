Welcome to Ponderosa Retreat

Nestled in the heart of the mountains, Ponderosa Retreat is your cozy escape into nature's embrace. Inspired by the serene beauty of Ponderosa Pine forests, our retreat invites you to unwind and savor the moment. Whether you're a local or a traveler seeking refuge, Ponderosa Retreat is here to welcome you. Join us for a warm drink in our café or join us in our nature workshops, and immerse yourself in the beauty of the mountains.

Ponderosa Café

Our rustic café invites you to unwind in a warm, inviting atmosphere. With seasonal specials and a focus on sustainability, we're dedicated to providing a delightful experience for locals and visitors alike. Come for the stunning scenery, stay for the connection and comfort!

Our Story

Over the years, Ponderosa Retreat has evolved into a vibrant community hub, hosting workshops and events that invite creativity and exploration. Today, we continue to thrive, honoring our roots while adapting to the needs of our guests. Join us and experience the serenity and inspiration that Ponderosa Retreat has to offer.

Join our guided hikes

Join our guided mountain hikes as part of our nature workshops! Experience breathtaking landscapes, enhance your outdoor skills, and connect with fellow nature enthusiasts. Perfect for all levels, these workshops offer a unique blend of adventure and education. Come explore the mountains with us.
